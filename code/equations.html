Human Phenotype Ontology

Let us denote:

  • $$D$$ as the set of diseases.

  • $$ \text{R}(g,p,d) $$ as a binary matrix where 1 indicates a relationship between gene $$g$$ and phenotype $$ p $$ in disease $$d$$, and 0 indicates no relationship.

  • $$ \text{E}(g,d) $$ as the mean evidence score for a relationship between gene $$ g $$ and disease $$d$$.

  • $$ M _{g,p} $$ as the mean evidence score for gene $$g$$ and phenotype $$p$$.

The final evidence-weighted gene x phenotype matrix $$ M $$ can be expressed as:

\[ M _{g,p} = \frac{\sum_{d \in D} \text{R}(g,p,d) \times \text{E}(g,d)}{\sum_{d \in D} \text{R}(g,p,d)} \]
